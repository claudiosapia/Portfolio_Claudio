About Me

Who am I?

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer and Designer based in Edinburgh. I am motivated, enthusiastic and well organised, with a keen eye for creative and eye-catching design. I love to create user-friendly and efficient websites that solve real-world problems.

I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as experience with popular web development frameworks such as React and Node.js. I am also familiar with database technologies such as MySQL and MongoDB.

I have professional experience as a Junior Designer and Developer and currently working as a Junior Designer and Developer at Lynchpin Media.

Aside from my technical skills, I am a great communicator and team player. I enjoy collaborating with others to create solutions that meet the needs of both users and businesses. I am also a quick learner and always eager to expand my knowledge and skills.

In addition to my studies at Edinburgh College and IT Professional Training, I am currently working as a volunteer web developer for Transition Edinburgh and Our Future Edinburgh. This is giving me the opportunity to gain valuable experience in working collaboratively in a team environment, managing projects, and delivering high-quality results.

I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to grow as a developer. In addition to my current studies, I have completed several online courses and tutorials to further develop my skills. I am excited about the opportunity to work with you and bring your vision to life through a custom website that effectively represents your brand and connects with your target audience.
